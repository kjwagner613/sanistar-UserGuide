Product Decompose

The system tracks parts consumption and knows exactly what components are needed for each build.

It uses a file called a BOM (Bill of Materials) to define the relationships.

When a sales order is submitted, it decomposes the products to a schedule or parts that are kept in inventory.

If there is not sufficient inventory a deficiency will be sent, to Greg, letting him know. You can also see deficiencies on the dashboard.

Deficiency e-Mail

On this email you can see that 10 S-CONDUIT must be ordered to be able to fulfill this sale.
