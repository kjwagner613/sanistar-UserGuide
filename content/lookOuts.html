Refresh Notice

This is letting you know that since the last time you accessed the app, some changes have occurred. Whether a server side update or structural, and the version on your workstation is no longer aligned.

Action Required:

All you must do is accept the refresh of your workstation's data. Then you will be up to data.

Consent Approval Notice

This message appears because the app has been updated or republished, and it needs your approval to reconnect to Microsoft 365 services it uses — such as SharePoint, Outlook, or Dataverse.

These connections may have been previously approved, but changes to the app or your session require re‑authorization.

Action Required:

Click "Allow" to re‑establish the necessary connections and continue using the app.
